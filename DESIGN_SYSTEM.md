# CircularOS Design System

## Core Aesthetic
- **Vibe:** Masculine, editorial, minimal, intelligent, technical, expensive, calm, precise, modern, institutional.
- **Backgrounds:** Predominantly white (`#FFFFFF`) and off-white (`#FAFAFC`).
- **Brand Color:** Purplish-blue (`#635BFF`) used for primary focus and interaction.
- **Accents:** Semantic colors for status, and subtle deep indigo for critical architectural nodes.
- **Layout:** "Pinterest-inspired" minimal workspace. No giant rounded cards, no glassmorphism, no neon glows.
- **Rule of Thumb:** 75% white/neutrals, 15% neutral typography/borders/surfaces, 7% brand purple, 3% semantic accents.

## Typography (Geist / Inter)
- **Display:** 56px - 64px (Marketing surfaces)
- **Page Title:** 30px - 36px
- **Section Title:** 20px - 24px
- **Card Title:** 15px - 17px
- **Body:** 14px - 16px
- **Metadata:** 12px - 13px (Use Geist Mono for IDs/technical hashes)
- **Weights:** 400-500 UI text, 500-600 important labels, 600-700 major headings. Avoid 800-900.

## Color Tokens
- **Background (Primary):** `#FFFFFF`
- **Background (Secondary):** `#FAFAFC`

- **Surface (Hover):** `#F3F2FA`
- **Surface (Selected):** `#EFEDFF`
- **Surface (Strong Selected):** `#E7E3FF`

- **Text (Secondary):** `#5E5A6B`
- **Text (Muted):** `#8C8799`

## Spacing & Structure
- **Horizontal Padding:** 24px minimum (32px-40px on large screens)
- **Section Spacing:** 32px - 48px
- **Component Spacing:** 16px - 24px
- **Card Padding:** 16px - 24px
- **Border Radius:** Controls 6-8px, Buttons/Inputs 8-10px, Cards 12-16px, Large Containers 16-20px max.

## Component Rules
- **Buttons:** Primary (`#635BFF` bg, white text, 8-10px radius). Secondary (white bg, dark text, `#DED9E8` border).
- **Cards:** Minimal borders, white bg, no heavy shadows. 
- **Tables:** White bg, subtle horizontal separators, minimal vertical borders. Sticky headers.
- **Badges:** Compact, tinted backgrounds (never fully saturated).
- **Shadows:** Very subtle shadow for small elevation, medium soft for floating panels. Most surfaces use borders instead of shadows.
